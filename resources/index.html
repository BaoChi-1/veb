About us

Hate the idea of dining alone (or straight up just want more company)? At this super cute themed café, you can sit with our popular children’s anime featuring the adventures of the eponymous white bear, a polar bear, Moomin and Molang. And also with their various pals to accompany you, literally, as the giant plush Moomin, white bear, Molang and other toys will be seated right across, or beside you as you sip on a tasty cuppa latte. Enjoy free-flow bread from their bakery, and hearty European-Japanese fare. Rather popular with the local folks as well, the café sees long, snaking queues. So be prepared for waiting time to dine with your favourite white bear, Moomin and Molang pals. Open daily from 10 am – 10 pm.
